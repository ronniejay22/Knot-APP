## 1. Who we are and what this policy covers

Knot is a mobile app that helps you plan thoughtful gifts, dates, and experiences for
your romantic partner. To do that, you tell Knot about your partner's preferences and
important dates, and Knot uses that information — together with artificial
intelligence and third-party data sources — to generate personalized suggestions.

This policy covers information we process when you use the Knot iOS app and our
backend service. It does not cover the practices of third parties we link to (such as
the merchants and booking sites where you complete a purchase), which have their own
privacy policies.

## 2. Information we collect

We collect the following categories of information, almost all of which you provide
to us directly.

### a. Account and identity information
- **Email address**, obtained through your chosen sign-in method (Sign in with Apple,
  Google Sign-In, or an email magic link). If you use Apple's Private Relay, we
  receive a relay email rather than your personal address.
- A **user identifier** created for your account by our authentication provider.

### b. Partner profile ("Vault")
The details you enter about your partner so we can personalize suggestions:
- Partner's **name**;
- **Relationship details** such as how long you have been together and whether you
  live together, separately, or long-distance;
- **Location** (city, state, and country) used to find local recommendations;
- **Interests and dislikes**;
- **Love languages**;
- Aesthetic **"vibes"** / style preferences;
- **Budget ranges** for different occasions.

### c. Important dates ("Milestones")
Birthdays, anniversaries, holidays, and custom occasions you add, including the date,
type, and whether the occasion recurs.

### d. "Hints"
Free-text notes (up to 500 characters each) that you write about your partner's
preferences, wishes, or things they've mentioned. To power search across your hints,
we also generate and store a mathematical representation (a "vector embedding") of the
hint text.

### e. Recommendations and feedback
The suggestions the Service generates for you, and your interactions with them — for
example ratings, saves, and whether you selected, refreshed, or dismissed a
suggestion. We use this to improve the relevance of future recommendations.

### f. Device and technical information
- An **Apple Push Notification service (APNs) device token** and your device platform,
  so we can send you the milestone reminders you enable;
- Your **notification preferences and time zone** (for example, quiet hours);
- Basic technical information necessarily exchanged when your app communicates with
  our servers over the internet.

### What we do **not** collect

To be clear about our data minimization:
- We do **not** access your device's **contacts, calendar, camera, photo library,
  precise/device location, or microphone**. The app does not request these
  permissions.
- We do **not** collect or store **payment card or bank information**. Knot does not
  process payments; purchases happen directly on a merchant's own website.
- We do **not** use third-party **advertising or analytics/tracking SDKs** (for
  example, Facebook, Firebase Analytics, Amplitude, or Mixpanel) in the app.
- We do **not** sell your personal information (see Section 6).

## 3. Information about your partner and other people

Much of the information you provide is **about another person** — your partner. You
are responsible for ensuring you have a lawful and appropriate basis to provide that
information and to use Knot to plan for that person. Please only enter information you
are comfortable and permitted to share, and do not enter sensitive information about
anyone that is not necessary for the Service.

## 4. How we use your information

We use the information described above to:
- **Provide and personalize the Service** — build your partner profile and generate
  tailored gift, date, experience, and idea recommendations;
- **Power search and matching** across your hints and your partner's profile;
- **Send milestone reminders and notifications** you have enabled, respecting your
  quiet hours;
- **Operate, secure, and maintain** your account and our systems, including
  preventing fraud and abuse and debugging issues;
- **Improve the Service**, including the quality and relevance of recommendations;
- **Comply with legal obligations** and enforce our Terms of Service.

## 5. Artificial intelligence processing

Generating personalized suggestions requires sending some of your information to
AI providers that act as our service providers:
- **Anthropic (Claude)** — we send relevant parts of your partner profile and hints
  to generate recommendations, creative ideas, search results, and milestone
  briefings.
- **Google Vertex AI** — we send your hint text to generate the vector embeddings
  used for search.

We send this data solely to produce results for you. We do not sell your personal
information to these providers, and we rely on their commitments to process the data
on our behalf. These providers may retain data only as needed to provide their
services to us and as described in their own terms.

## 6. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context
behavioral advertising. We share information only in the following circumstances:

### a. Service providers
We use trusted third parties to run the Service. They may process your information
only to provide services to us:

| Provider | Purpose | Information involved |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Supabase | Database and authentication (stores your account and Vault data) | Account and all app data |
| Vercel | Hosting for our backend | Data in transit through our backend |
| Anthropic (Claude) | AI recommendations, ideas, search, and briefings | Partner profile, hints, occasion context |
| Google Cloud / Vertex AI | Hint embeddings for search | Hint text |
| Upstash (QStash) | Scheduling milestone notifications | Notification scheduling data |

| OpenTable / Resy | Restaurant reservation links (affiliate) | Location, occasion context |
| Firecrawl | Curated content discovery | Interests, vibes |

We share only the information reasonably necessary for each provider's function; we
do not send providers your full account record unless required for their role.

### b. Merchants and affiliate links
Recommendations may link to third-party merchants and booking sites (for example,
Amazon or a restaurant reservation platform). When you follow a link and complete a
purchase or booking, that transaction is between you and the merchant, under the
merchant's own terms and privacy policy. Some links are **affiliate links**, meaning
we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you (see our Terms of Service).

### c. Legal and safety
We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or legal process, or where
we believe in good faith it is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the
safety of others.

### d. Business transfers
If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, your
information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will require the
recipient to honor this Privacy Policy or provide notice of any material change.

## 7. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your
account, we begin a **60-day grace period** during which the data is retained so you
can restore the account if you change your mind. After the grace period ends, your
account and associated data are **permanently deleted** from our systems (including
via automatic cascade deletion of your Vault, hints, recommendations, feedback, and
notifications). We may retain limited information where required for legal, security,
or accounting purposes.

## 8. Your rights and choices

Regardless of where you live, Knot gives you the following controls:
- **Access and export** — you can export a copy of your data as a JSON file from
  within the app;
- **Correction** — you can edit your partner profile, hints, and milestones at any
  time in the app;
- **Deletion** — you can delete your account from within the app; deletion follows the
  60-day restore window described above, after which data is permanently removed;
- **Restore** — you can cancel a pending deletion within the grace period;
- **Notifications** — you can turn notifications on or off and set quiet hours;
- **Sign out** — signing out clears your session from the device's secure storage.

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under laws such as the EU
and UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or the California Consumer Privacy Act
as amended (CCPA/CPRA) — including the right to access, correct, delete, or port your
information, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to not be discriminated
against for exercising these rights. Because we do not sell personal information or
use it for cross-context behavioral advertising, there is no "sale" or "sharing" for
you to opt out of. To exercise any right, contact us at the address in Section 13. We
will not discriminate against you for making a request.

## 9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:
- Data is encrypted **in transit** using HTTPS/TLS;
- Data is encrypted **at rest** by our database provider;
- Each user's data is isolated at the database layer so you can only access your own
  information;
- Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure **Keychain**.

Please note that Knot processes your information in unencrypted form on our servers in
order to generate suggestions (we do not use end-to-end encryption). No method of
transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute
security.
